<p><p>The market for Electronic Liquid Optically Clear Adhesive (LOCA) can be categorized into two types: Resistive OCA and Capacitive OCA. Resistive OCA is a type of adhesive used in resistive touch screens, where pressure applied by a finger or stylus helps to create electrical contact between two separate layers. On the other hand, Capacitive OCA is utilized in capacitive touch screens, which detect the conductive properties of an object (like a finger) to determine touch input. These market types cater to different touchscreen technologies, offering diverse solutions for various electronic devices.</p></p>
