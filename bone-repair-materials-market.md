<p><ul><li>Bone Graft</li><li>Bone Cement</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Bone repair materials market includes two main types: bone graft and bone cement. Bone graft is a surgical procedure that involves transplanting bone tissue or synthetic material to repair and regenerate bones affected by injury or disease. It promotes bone healing by providing a scaffold for new bone growth. On the other hand, bone cement is a type of adhesive used during orthopedic surgeries, especially joint replacements. It is injected into the bone to fill gaps and secure implants, enhancing stability and promoting bone fusion. Both bone graft and bone cement play crucial roles in the field of bone repair and regeneration.</p></p>
