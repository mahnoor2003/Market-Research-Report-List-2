<p><p>Electric cheese graters are available in two types: automatic and semi-automatic. Automatic graters feature a fully automated process where the cheese is placed into the machine, and it is then grated without any additional effort required. These are suitable for commercial kitchens or large-scale cheese production. On the other hand, semi-automatic graters require some manual input from the user. The cheese is generally placed into the grater manually, and the machine takes care of the grating process. These are commonly used in home kitchens or smaller food establishments.</p></p>
