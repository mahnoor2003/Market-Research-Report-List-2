<p><p>The automated pool cleaner market consists of two main types: the crawler drive and the wheel drive. Crawler drive cleaners move around the pool by using tracks or treads, similar to a tank, allowing them to navigate various pool surfaces and obstacles. Wheel drive cleaners, on the other hand, use wheels to move around the pool and clean the surface. Both types of cleaners are designed to autonomously clean pools, but they differ in their movement mechanism, offering options to cater to different pool layouts and cleaning needs.</p></p>
