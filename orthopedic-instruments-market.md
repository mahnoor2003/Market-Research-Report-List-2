<p><p>The orthopedic instruments market consists of three main types of products: trauma products, spine products, and joint products. Trauma products are used for treating fractures, injuries, and other traumatic conditions in the musculoskeletal system. Spine products are designed for addressing conditions related to the spine, such as herniated discs or spinal deformities. Joint products are focused on the treatment of conditions affecting the joints, including arthritis or joint instability. These different types of instruments cater to the specific needs of patients and enable orthopedic surgeons to provide appropriate interventions for various orthopedic conditions.</p></p>
