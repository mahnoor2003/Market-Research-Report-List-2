<p><strong>The Semiconductor Pressure Sensor Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Piezoresistive Sensors</li><li>PN Junction Sensors</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Semiconductor pressure sensors are used to measure pressure in various applications. Piezoresistive sensors are a type of semiconductor pressure sensor that utilize the piezoresistive effect, which means their electrical resistance changes when exposed to pressure. They offer advantages such as high accuracy, small size, and low cost. PN junction sensors, on the other hand, utilize the principle of PN junction diodes to measure pressure. They are typically made of silicon and rely on the change in voltage across the PN junction under pressure to determine the pressure level.</p></p>
