<p><ul><li>Plane Electronic Compass</li><li>Three Dimensional Electronic Compass</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The electronic compass market consists of two main types: plane electronic compass and three-dimensional electronic compass. A plane electronic compass provides directional information within a single plane, such as north, south, east, or west. It is commonly used in navigation systems and automotive applications. On the other hand, a three-dimensional electronic compass provides information in multiple dimensions, including pitch, roll, and yaw, making it suitable for applications where accurate orientation in three-dimensional space is required, such as aviation and virtual reality.</p></p>
