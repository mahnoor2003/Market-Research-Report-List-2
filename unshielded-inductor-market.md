<p><p>The unshielded inductor market is comprised of two major types: ferrite drum surface mount and radial lead through hole. Ferrite drum surface mount inductors are compact and are designed to be mounted directly onto a circuit board, making them suitable for smaller electronic devices. Radial lead through hole inductors, on the other hand, have leads that are inserted through holes in the circuit board, providing a secure electrical connection. These inductors are commonly used in applications that require higher power and have larger dimensions due to their through-hole construction.</p></p>
