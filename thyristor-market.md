<p><p>The Thyristor market consists of two main types: Unidirectional Thyristor and Bidirectional Thyristor. Unidirectional Thyristors conduct current only in one direction, making them suitable for applications like rectification and power control. Bidirectional Thyristors, on the other hand, can conduct current in both directions, allowing for versatile applications such as AC power control and motor control. Both types play a crucial role in various industries, including power generation, industrial automation, and consumer electronics, providing reliable and efficient electrical switching capabilities.</p></p>
