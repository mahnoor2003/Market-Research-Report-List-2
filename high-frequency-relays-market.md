<p><p>The High Frequency Relays market can be categorized into two types: Surface Mount High Frequency Relays and Through Hole High Frequency Relays. Surface Mount High Frequency Relays are smaller in size and can be directly mounted on the surface of a circuit board, making them suitable for compact electronic devices. Through Hole High Frequency Relays, on the other hand, require holes to be drilled in the circuit board for mounting, and are typically larger in size. Both types serve the purpose of controlling high-frequency signals in various electronic applications.</p></p>
