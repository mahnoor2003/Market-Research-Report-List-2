<p><p>The residential faucets market includes three main types of faucets: one-hand mixers, pillars, and two-hand mixers. One-hand mixers are faucets that allow users to control both the temperature and water flow with a single lever. Pillar faucets have separate handles for hot and cold water control. Two-hand mixers are similar to one-hand mixers but have two separate handles for temperature and water flow control. These different faucet types cater to the varying preferences and needs of residential consumers when it comes to controlling water temperature and flow in their homes.</p></p>
