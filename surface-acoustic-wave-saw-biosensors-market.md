<p><strong>The Surface Acoustic Wave (SAW) Biosensors Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Delay-line type</li><li>Resonators Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Surface Acoustic Wave (SAW) Biosensors Market includes two primary types: Delay-line type and Resonators Type. The Delay-line type biosensors use a delay line to propagate Surface Acoustic Waves and detect changes in the wave due to the sample being tested. These biosensors are commonly used in healthcare and environmental monitoring applications. On the other hand, Resonators Type biosensors utilize resonating structures to detect changes in frequency caused by the sample under test. These biosensors find applications in gas sensing and chemical analysis.</p></p>
