<p><ul><li>Pneumatical Type</li><li>Hydraulical Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The aircraft carbon braking system market consists of two types: pneumatical and hydraulical. The pneumatical type uses compressed air to operate the brake system, providing efficient stopping power and quick response times. On the other hand, the hydraulical type uses hydraulic fluid to activate the brakes, ensuring smooth and precise control. Both types are essential in the aviation industry, with the pneumatical type being widely used in smaller aircraft and the hydraulical type preferred for larger commercial and military aircraft.</p></p>
