<p><ul><li>Bilayer Lithium-Ion Battery Separator</li><li>Trilayer Lithium-Ion Battery Separator</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The lithium-ion battery separator market includes two key types: bilayer and trilayer lithium-ion battery separators. Bilayer separators consist of two layers that help prevent internal short circuits in lithium-ion batteries, thus enhancing their safety and reliability. On the other hand, trilayer separators have an additional layer that enhances their thermal durability and chemical stability. Both types are crucial components in lithium-ion batteries, ensuring a controlled flow of lithium ions between the cathode and anode, which is necessary for their effective functioning.</p></p>
