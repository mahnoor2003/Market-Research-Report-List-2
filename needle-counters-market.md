<p><p>Needle counters are medical devices used to accurately count the number of surgical needles used during a procedure. They come in two main types: Double Magnet and Single Magnet. In Double Magnet needle counters, both the needle and the needle driver have magnets, allowing for easy and precise counting. Single Magnet needle counters only have a magnet in the needle driver, requiring the user to touch the needle against it for counting. Both types ensure accurate needle counting, but Double Magnet counters provide a more efficient and convenient counting experience.</p></p>
