<p><p>The ROV Tether Management System market consists of two major types: Electric Observation Class ROVs and Hydraulic Work Class ROVs. Electric Observation Class ROVs are used for visual inspection and data gathering purposes, typically in shallow water environments. They are powered by electric motors and are compact in size. On the other hand, Hydraulic Work Class ROVs are larger and more robust, designed for heavy-duty tasks like construction and subsea maintenance. They utilize hydraulic systems for power and are capable of operating in deeper waters.</p></p>
