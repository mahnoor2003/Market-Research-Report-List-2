<p><p>The high purity diatomaceous earth market can be classified into two main types: food grade and filter grade. Food grade diatomaceous earth is primarily used in agriculture and food production due to its low crystalline silica content. It is commonly added to animal feed as an anti-caking agent and used in organic farming. On the other hand, filter grade diatomaceous earth is utilized for filtration purposes, such as water and air filtration systems. Its high porosity and microscopic openings make it effective in trapping impurities and ensuring clean filtration.</p></p>
