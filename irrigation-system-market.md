<p><p>The irrigation system market consists of various types, namely center pivot, lateral move, solid set, and others. Center pivot systems utilize a central pivot point to move water through a series of sprinklers in a circular pattern. Lateral move systems move water through a linear path, often in the shape of an "L" or "U." Solid set systems involve stationary sprinklers that are manually positioned. Other types of irrigation systems include drip irrigation, surface irrigation, and subsurface irrigation, each with unique characteristics and applications. These systems help efficiently distribute water in agriculture, landscaping, and other industries.</p></p>
