They are used in various applications like telecommunications, avionics, and military equipment. There are two types of TCVCXO markets- Indirect Compensated Crystal Oscillators and Direct Compensated Crystal Oscillators. Indirect Compensated Crystal Oscillators use a digital-to-frequency converter to modify the oscillator frequency, ensuring temperature stability. Direct Compensated Crystal Oscillators, on the other hand, include a temperature sensing element that directly compensates for the temperature-induced frequency changes, providing highly accurate and stable output frequencies. Both types cater to different requirements and offer temperature compensation solutions in their respective markets.</p></p>
