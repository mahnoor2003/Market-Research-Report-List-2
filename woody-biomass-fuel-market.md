<p><p>The Woody Biomass Fuel market consists of two main types: Bulk Biomass Briquettes and Biomass Pellets. Bulk Biomass Briquettes are produced by compressing woody biomass materials into dense blocks, often used for heating applications in industrial settings. On the other hand, Biomass Pellets are manufactured by processing woody biomass into small, cylindrical pellets, commonly used as a renewable energy source in residential heating systems and power plants. Both types offer sustainable alternatives to fossil fuels, contributing to the reduction of greenhouse gas emissions and promoting a greener energy future.</p></p>
