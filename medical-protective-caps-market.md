<p><p>The Medical Protective Caps market is divided into two main types: Disposable Protective Caps and Reusable Protective Caps. Disposable Protective Caps are designed for single-use in healthcare settings to prevent cross-contamination and are disposed of after each use. On the other hand, Reusable Protective Caps are made from durable materials and can be cleaned and sterilized for multiple uses. Both types serve the purpose of providing a protective barrier for the head and hair in medical environments, but differ in their intended lifespan and sustainability.</p></p>
