<p><p>High-Capacity Fluid-Bed Dryers are used in various industries to dry wet materials. There are two types of such dryers: Static Fluid-bed Dryers and Vibrating Fluid-bed Dryers. </p><p>Static Fluid-bed Dryers consist of a stationary bed of material that is fluidized by the upward flow of gas. This type is commonly used for fine particles and heat-sensitive materials. </p><p>Vibrating Fluid-bed Dryers have a vibrating bed that fluidizes the material, allowing for better heat transfer and drying efficiency. They are suitable for larger particles and materials that require intense drying.</p></p>
