This growth can be attributed to various factors including increasing healthcare expenditure, rising demand for medical devices, technological advancements, shifting focus of medical device companies towards core activities, and the need for cost-effective solutions. One such trend is the growing preference for contract research organizations (CROs) as outsourcing partners for medical device companies. CROs provide services such as clinical research, regulatory consulting, and post-market surveillance, which are essential for gaining regulatory approvals and ensuring product safety and efficacy.</p><p>Another emerging trend is the shift towards nearshore and offshore outsourcing destinations.
