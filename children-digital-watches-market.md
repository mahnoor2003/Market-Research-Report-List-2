<p><p>The children's digital watch market can be categorized into three types - digital quartz, pointer quartz, and others. Digital quartz watches display the time in digital form using quartz technology, offering accuracy and simplicity. Pointer quartz watches, on the other hand, utilize both digital and analog mechanisms to indicate time, providing a traditional feel with a combination of hour and minute hands alongside a digital display. The "others" category includes various alternative technologies or unique designs that don't fit into the aforementioned types, offering diverse options for children's watch preferences.</p></p>
