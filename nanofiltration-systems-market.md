Spiral membrane systems use a spiral configuration to separate particles, ions, and organic impurities from water, while tubular membrane systems use a tube-shaped structure for separation. Both types offer high efficiency and excellent contaminant removal capabilities. These systems are widely used in industries such as food and beverage, pharmaceuticals, and chemical processing for clean water production and purification.</p></p>
