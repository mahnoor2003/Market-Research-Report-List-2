<p><p>The Coronary Angiography Devices Market is categorized into different types based on the imaging techniques used. MR Angiography utilizes magnetic resonance imaging to capture high-resolution images. Other types encompass emerging technologies such as optical coherence tomography and intravascular ultrasound. These different types provide healthcare professionals with various imaging options to assess coronary artery health and diagnose conditions such as blockages or narrowing, enabling precise treatment decisions.</p></p>
