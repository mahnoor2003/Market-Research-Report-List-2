<p><strong>Market Segmentation</strong></p>
<p><strong>The Leaded Inductors Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Axial</li><li>Radial</li><li>Through Hole</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The leaded inductors market comprises three types: axial, radial, and through hole. Axial inductors have their leads extending from opposite ends, making them suitable for through-hole mounting on circuit boards. Radial inductors, on the other hand, have both leads extending from the same side, allowing for convenient placement in circuits where space is limited. Through-hole inductors have long leads that can be inserted into pre-drilled holes on circuit boards, providing stability and secure attachment. These different types cater to various mounting requirements and facilitate efficient integration into electronic systems.</p></p>
