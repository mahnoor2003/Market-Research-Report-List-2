<p><p>Surface disinfection products can be categorized into three main types: liquid disinfection products, spray disinfection products, and wipe disinfection products. </p><p>Liquid disinfection products are typically used by pouring them onto a surface and spreading them evenly. These products are commonly used in healthcare settings for larger surface areas.</p><p>Spray disinfection products come in aerosol form and are sprayed directly onto surfaces. They are popular for their convenience and ability to reach difficult areas. These products are commonly used in households and public spaces.</p><p>Wipe disinfection products are pre-moistened wipes that are used for wiping surfaces. They are convenient and portable, making them preferred for personal use and on-the-go disinfection.</p></p>
