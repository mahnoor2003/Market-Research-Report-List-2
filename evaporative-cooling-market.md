<p><strong>Market Segmentation</strong></p>
<p><strong>The Evaporative Cooling Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Direct Evaporative Cooling</li><li>Indirect Evaporative Cooling</li><li>Two-stage Evaporative Cooling</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Evaporative cooling is a process that uses the evaporation of water to reduce temperatures. There are three main types of evaporative cooling markets. </p><p>Direct evaporative cooling involves passing hot, dry air through water-soaked pads, causing the water to evaporate and lower the air's temperature. </p><p>Indirect evaporative cooling utilizes a heat exchanger to cool the air without adding humidity by transferring heat to a separate water circuit. </p><p>Two-stage evaporative cooling combines both direct and indirect methods, first cooling the air through direct evaporation and then passing it through a heat exchanger to further reduce the temperature.</p></p>
