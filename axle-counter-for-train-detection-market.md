<p><ul><li>Rail Side Axle Counter</li><li>On-rail Axle Counter</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Axle counters are used for train detection, and there are different market types available. Rail side axle counters are installed beside the railway track and work by detecting the axles as the train passes by. They provide accurate and reliable train detection. On-rail axle counters, on the other hand, are installed directly on the rail and use sensors to detect the passing axles. Both types of axle counters are important for train detection systems, ensuring the safe and efficient operation of railways.</p></p>
