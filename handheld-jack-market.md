<p><p>A handheld jack, commonly known as a bottle jack or hydraulic jack, is a mechanical device used to lift heavy loads such as vehicles or machinery. Handheld jacks are widely used in automotive repair shops, construction sites, and workshops due to their compact size, portability, and ease of use.</p><p>The handheld jack market is witnessing significant growth, driven by several factors. The increasing demand for automotive repair and maintenance services, coupled with the growing automotive industry, is a major driver of market growth.
