<p>&nbsp;</p>
<p><p>The microtunneling machine market consists of various types of machines used for underground tunneling projects. Earth Pressure Balance Machines (EPB) use the excavated material to balance the pressure of the surrounding soil. Slurry Shield (SS) machines utilize a pressurized slurry to stabilize the tunnel face during excavation. Pilot Tube Microtunneling (PTMT) involves manually guided boring machines. Auger Boring machines use rotating blades to cut through soil. Vacuum Extraction TBM utilizes suction to remove soil during excavation. Other types of machines include pipe jacking, rock tunneling, or hybrid machines that combine different techniques for specific projects.</p></p>
