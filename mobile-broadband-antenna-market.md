<p><p>The mobile broadband antenna market consists of two major types: LTE antennas and 5G antennas. LTE antennas are designed to support the Long-Term Evolution (LTE) technology, which provides high-speed wireless communication. They enable faster data transfer rates and improved network reliability. On the other hand, 5G antennas are specifically built to support the fifth-generation (5G) technology. These antennas offer even higher data speeds and lower latency compared to LTE antennas. Both LTE and 5G antennas play a crucial role in enhancing mobile broadband connectivity and improving the overall communication experience.</p></p>
