<p><ul><li>Cloud-based</li><li>On-premises</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Payroll software market types include cloud-based and on-premises solutions. Cloud-based software is hosted on remote servers and accessible through the internet, enabling users to access and manage payroll information from any location. It offers flexibility, scalability, and automatic updates, reducing the need for IT infrastructure. On-premises software, on the other hand, is installed and maintained locally on the company's servers, allowing for greater control and customization. However, it requires dedicated IT resources and lacks the convenience and accessibility of cloud-based solutions.</p></p>
