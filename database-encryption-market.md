<p><strong>The Database Encryption Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Transparent Encryption</li><li>Column-level Encryption</li><li>File-system Encryption</li><li>Application- Level Encryption</li><li>Key Management</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Database Encryption Market comprises several types of encryption methods. Transparent encryption involves encrypting and decrypting data automatically without affecting user operations. Column-level encryption focuses on encrypting specific columns, ensuring only authorized users can access the data. File-system encryption encrypts entire file systems, reducing the risk of unauthorized access. Application-level encryption secures specific applications or software systems. Key Management is a vital market segment that involves managing encryption keys, including their creation, storage, distribution, and destruction, ensuring secure communication and data protection.</p></p>
