PVC insulation tapes are designed for electrical insulation and are resistant to moisture and chemicals. PET tapes, made from polyethylene terephthalate, are commonly used for packaging and sealing purposes. Labels are adhesive tapes used for identification and information purposes. Double-sided tapes have adhesive on both sides and are used for bonding two surfaces together.
