<p><p>The inflatable life vests market consists of two main types: manual inflatable life vests and auto inflatable life vests. Manual inflatable life vests require the user to manually activate the inflation mechanism to inflate the vest. These vests are typically inflated by pulling a cord or using a CO2 cartridge. On the other hand, auto inflatable life vests automatically inflate upon contact with water. They have a built-in sensor that detects water immersion and triggers the inflation mechanism. Both types of life vests are designed to provide buoyancy and ensure the safety of individuals in water.</p></p>
