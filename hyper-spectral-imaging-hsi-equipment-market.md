VNIR HSI captures images in the visible and near-infrared regions, suitable for analyzing agriculture and environmental applications. SWIR HSI operates in the short wave infrared region and is ideal for surveillance, mineralogy, and chemistry studies. MWIR HSI captures images in the medium wave infrared region, commonly used in defense and industrial applications. LWIR HSI operates in the long wave infrared region, providing thermal imaging for medical and security purposes. The Other market includes HSI equipment designed for specific niches or industry-specific applications.</p></p>
