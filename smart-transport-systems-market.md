<p><p>Smart Transport Systems (STS) include a variety of technologies that aim to improve efficiency, safety, and sustainability in transportation. Advanced Traffic Management Systems (ATMS) enable real-time traffic monitoring, signal optimization, and incident management. Advanced Transportation Pricing System (ATPS) employs electronic tolling, congestion pricing, and dynamic pricing to manage traffic flow and reduce congestion. Advanced Traveler Information System (ATIS) provides real-time travel information to optimize route planning and enhance traveler experience. Advanced Public Transportation System (APTS) integrates technologies like fare collection, fleet management, and passenger information to enhance public transit services. Other STS include intelligent parking systems, electric vehicle charging infrastructure, and freight management systems.</p></p>
