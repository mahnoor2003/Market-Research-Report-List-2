<p><p>The stretch wrapping equipment market comprises two main types: fully automatic and semi-automatic machines. Fully automatic stretch wrapping machines are designed to perform the entire wrapping process without any human intervention. They are efficient and suitable for high-volume production lines. On the other hand, semi-automatic stretch wrapping machines require some manual input, such as loading and unloading the pallets. These machines are more cost-effective and can be used for low to medium production volumes. Both types of machines are widely used in industries for securing and protecting products during transportation or storage.</p></p>
