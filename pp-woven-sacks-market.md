<p><p>PP woven sacks are a type of packaging made from polypropylene plastic material. They come in two main market types: laminated PP woven sacks and non-laminated PP woven sacks. Laminated PP woven sacks are coated with a layer of protective film, offering increased resistance to moisture, UV radiation, and damage. On the other hand, non-laminated PP woven sacks lack this protective layer but are still durable and cost-effective. Both types of sacks are widely used in industries such as agriculture, construction, and food packaging.</p></p>
