<p><strong>The Raman Spectroscopy Technology Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>

<p><p>Raman spectroscopy technology market includes various types such as surface-enhanced Raman scattering (SERS) technology, tip-enhanced Raman scattering (TERS) technology, and others. SERS technology enhances Raman signals by using nanostructured metal surfaces, enabling sensitive and accurate detection of molecules. TERS technology enhances Raman signals by employing a sharp metallic tip, enabling high-resolution imaging of nanoscale samples. The "others" category encompasses alternative or emerging technologies that utilize different methods to enhance Raman signals, including plasmonics, metamaterials, and graphene-based approaches. These technologies collectively offer advancements in materials analysis, chemical identification, and biomedical research.</p></p>
