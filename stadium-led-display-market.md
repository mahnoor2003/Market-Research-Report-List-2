<p><p>The stadium LED display market is categorized into two types: individually mounted and surface mounted. Individually mounted displays are composed of separate LED modules that are assembled together to form the larger display. Surface mounted displays, on the other hand, use a single large LED panel without separate modules. Individually mounted displays offer flexibility in terms of size and design, while surface mounted displays provide a seamless and uniform visual experience. Both types are widely used in stadiums and sports arenas to enhance the viewing experience for spectators.</p></p>
