<p><p>Optical networking and communication market consists of various types of equipment that enable the transmission and reception of optical signals for data communication. Transceivers function as both transmitter and receiver, converting electrical signals to optical signals and vice versa. Amplifiers improve optical signal strength for long-distance transmission. Switches allow routing of data between different optical paths. Splitters divide optical signals into multiple paths for distribution. Circulators enable unidirectional signal flow between different ports in an optical network. These equipment types collectively facilitate the efficient and reliable transmission of data through optical fibers.</p></p>
