<p><p>The cosmetic preservative market encompasses various types of preservatives. Paraben esters are a commonly used type that help prevent the growth of bacteria, yeast, and molds in cosmetic products. Formaldehyde donors are another type of preservative that slowly release small amounts of formaldehyde, which has antimicrobial properties. Phenol derivatives and alcohols, such as benzyl alcohol and phenoxyethanol, are also used as preservatives. Quaternary compounds, like benzalkonium chloride, are effective against bacteria, fungi, and viruses. Organic acids, like sorbic acid and benzoic acid, have antimicrobial properties and they are used as preservatives in cosmetics.</p></p>
