<p><strong>The IC Packaging Substrate (SUB) Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Organic Substrate</li><li>Inorganic Substrate</li><li>Composite Substrate</li></ul></p>
<p><p>IC Packaging Substrate (SUB) is a crucial component in the semiconductor industry. It can be classified into three types: organic, inorganic, and composite substrates. Organic substrates, made of materials like epoxy or BT resin, are cost-effective and suitable for low- to mid-range applications. Inorganic substrates, using ceramics like alumina or aluminum nitride, offer excellent thermal conductivity but are more expensive. Composite substrates combine elements of both organic and inorganic substrates, providing a balance between cost and performance. Each type has its own advantages, allowing manufacturers to choose the most appropriate substrate for their specific requirements.</p></p>
