<p><p>Laparoscopic scissors are surgical instruments used in minimally invasive procedures. There are two main types: monopolar and bipolar laparoscopic scissors. Monopolar scissors use high-frequency electrical energy to cut and coagulate tissue, while bipolar scissors have both cutting and coagulating capabilities. The monopolar scissors market focuses on instruments that use a single electrical current for cutting, while the bipolar scissors market emphasizes instruments that can simultaneously cut and coagulate tissue. Both types play a crucial role in laparoscopic procedures by allowing surgeons to perform precise and controlled tissue dissection.</p></p>
