<p><p>The PTFE (polytetrafluoroethylene) vascular grafts market is classified into two types: Large Diameter and Small Diameter. Large Diameter grafts are typically used in bypass surgeries as they have wider internal diameters, allowing for improved blood flow and compatibility with major blood vessels. On the other hand, Small Diameter grafts are used in peripheral and coronary artery bypass procedures, where they need to be smaller in size to fit narrow blood vessels. Both types serve crucial roles in vascular surgeries, depending on the specific requirements and location within the body.</p></p>
