<p><p>The disposable procedure mask market includes two main types: surgical masks and procedure masks. Surgical masks are primarily used by healthcare professionals during surgeries to protect themselves and patients from potential contamination. They have higher filtration efficiency and often include a fluid-resistant layer. Procedure masks, on the other hand, are also employed by healthcare workers but are more commonly used for routine procedures and examinations. They offer lower filtration efficiency compared to surgical masks but still provide some level of protection against respiratory droplets and splashes.</p></p>
