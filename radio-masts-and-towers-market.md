<p><p>The radio masts and towers market can be categorized into several types. Lattice tower refers to a structure consisting of a framework of crossed metal beams. Guyed tower is a tall structure supported by wires anchored to the ground, providing stability. Monopole tower is a single, tall pole used to support antennas. Stealth tower is designed to blend into its surroundings to minimize visual impact. Other market segments may include self-supporting towers, tripod towers, and rooftop towers, among others. These different tower types cater to diverse needs in the radio communication industry.</p></p>
