<p><p>The laboratory table market can be categorized into two main types: adjustable laboratory tables and fixed laboratory tables. Adjustable laboratory tables are designed with a height-adjustment mechanism, allowing users to customize the table's height according to their specific needs or ergonomic requirements. On the other hand, fixed laboratory tables have a fixed height and cannot be adjusted. The choice between these two types depends on the specific application and preferences of the users, as adjustable tables offer greater flexibility while fixed tables provide stability and durability.</p></p>
