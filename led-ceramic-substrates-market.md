<p><strong>The LED Ceramic Substrates Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Thin Film Substrates</li><li>Thick Film Substrates</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The LED Ceramic Substrates market includes two main types: Thin Film Substrates and Thick Film Substrates. Thin Film Substrates are made from a thin layer of ceramic material on a carrier material like glass or stainless steel. They offer excellent heat dissipation and stability, making them suitable for high-power LED applications. Thick Film Substrates, on the other hand, have a thicker ceramic layer and are more cost-effective. They are commonly used for low- to medium-power LED applications where heat dissipation requirements are less stringent.</p></p>
