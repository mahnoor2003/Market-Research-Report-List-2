<p><p>Bone imaging equipment market refers to the various types of medical devices used for diagnosing and monitoring bone-related conditions. X-ray systems use electromagnetic radiation to create images of bones. CT scanners use X-rays and computer technology to produce detailed cross-sectional images. MRI systems employ strong magnetic fields and radio waves to generate precise images. EOS imaging systems allow for 3D imaging with low radiation exposure. Ultrasound uses sound waves to create images of bones. Nuclear imaging systems use small amounts of radioactive substances for bone imaging.</p></p>
