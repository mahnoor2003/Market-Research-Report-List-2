<p><p>The VHF Marine Radio market consists of two main types: Fixed-Mount and Handheld radios. Fixed-Mount radios are permanently installed on boats and have a higher transmitting power, allowing for better long-distance communication. They are equipped with external antennas and are typically used by professional marine users. On the other hand, Handheld radios are portable devices that can be carried on board or taken ashore. They have a lower transmitting power and are commonly used by recreational boaters or for short-range communication. Both types play a crucial role in ensuring safety and communication on the water.</p></p>
