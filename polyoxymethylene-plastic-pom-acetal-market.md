<p><p>Polyoxymethylene plastic, also known as POM or Acetal, has two main market types: homopolymer POM and copolymer POM. Homopolymer POM is a type of POM that is made up of a single polymer. It offers high stiffness, strength, and dimensional stability, making it suitable for applications that require precision and durability, such as automotive parts, gears, and bearings. On the other hand, copolymer POM is a blend of two different polymers, offering improved chemical resistance and thermal stability. It is commonly used in industries like electronics, appliances, and packaging.</p></p>
