<p><p>The CPU embedded wireless LAN market includes two main types: microcontrollers and microprocessors. Microcontrollers are small, low-power devices that integrate a microprocessor core with memory and peripherals on a single chip. They are widely used in applications that require a simple processing unit, such as home automation systems and small IoT devices. They are commonly found in personal computers, smartphones, and other high-performance applications.</p></p>
