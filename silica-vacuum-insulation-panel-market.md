<p><p>Silica Vacuum Insulation Panel (VIP) is a high-performance insulating material that consists of a fumed silica core enclosed within a gas-tight envelope made of flexible polymer film. It provides superior thermal insulation by creating a vacuum inside the panel, which minimizes heat transfer through conduction and convection. These panels have a low thermal conductivity and are primarily used in the construction, refrigeration, and transportation industries.</p><p>The Silica Vacuum Insulation Panel Market is expected to witness significant growth in the coming years.
