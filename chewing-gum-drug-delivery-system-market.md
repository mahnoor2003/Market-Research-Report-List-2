<p><ul><li>Solid</li><li>Semi-solid</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The chewing gum drug delivery system market encompasses two main types: solid and semi-solid. Solid drug delivery systems involve the incorporation of medicine into the gum matrix, allowing for controlled release of drugs through chewing. This method is commonly used for delivering active ingredients such as analgesics, nicotine, or vitamins. On the other hand, semi-solid drug delivery systems involve the encapsulation of drugs within a liquid or gel center, which is then surrounded by a gum shell. This form of delivery system is often utilized for drugs that require protection or mask their taste.</p></p>
