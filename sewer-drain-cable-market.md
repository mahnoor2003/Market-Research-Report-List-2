<p><p>The Sewer Drain Cable market can be categorized into two types: Inner Core and No Core. Inner Core refers to the cables that have a central core made of solid material, usually a high-strength steel wire. This core provides strength and stability to the cable, allowing it to navigate through tough clogs in sewer drains. On the other hand, No Core cables are typically made of coiled wires without a central core. These cables are more flexible and are ideal for navigating through bends and curves in sewer pipes.</p></p>
