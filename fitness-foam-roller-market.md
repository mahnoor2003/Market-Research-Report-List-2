<p><p>The fitness foam roller market is segmented into various types such as EVA, PE, EPP, and others. EVA foam rollers are made from ethylene-vinyl acetate and are known for their durability and softness. PE foam rollers are made from polyethylene and are commonly used in physical therapy due to their firmness and resistance. EPP foam rollers are made from expanded polypropylene and are known for their high density and durability. The "others" category comprises foam rollers made from materials such as PVC or rubber. These foam rollers offer different levels of firmness and density, allowing users to choose the one that best suits their needs.</p></p>
