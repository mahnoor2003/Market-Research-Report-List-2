<p><ul><li>Atmospheric Manipulator</li><li>Vacuum Manipulator</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The wafer transfer robot market includes two main types: atmospheric manipulator and vacuum manipulator. An atmospheric manipulator is a robotic system used for wafer handling in atmospheric conditions, where the robot uses mechanical grippers or end-effectors to transfer wafers. On the other hand, a vacuum manipulator is designed to handle wafers in vacuum environments, utilizing a vacuum suction mechanism to securely hold and transfer the delicate wafers. These different types cater to the specific needs and requirements of wafer manufacturing and handling processes in different atmospheric and vacuum conditions.</p></p>
