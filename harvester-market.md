<p><p>Harvester market can be classified into two types: walking type and suspension type. </p><p>Walking type harvesters are operated by a walking mechanism that allows them to move through the field to harvest crops effectively. This type is suitable for areas with relatively flat terrains and where the soil is not excessively wet or muddy. </p><p>Suspension type harvesters, on the other hand, are equipped with a suspension system that enables them to operate in uneven terrains, such as hills or fields with uneven surfaces. This type is designed to provide better stability, maneuverability, and crop collection efficiency in challenging farming environments.</p></p>
