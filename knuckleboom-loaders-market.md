<p><p>Knuckleboom loaders are versatile hydraulic cranes used in various industries for lifting and handling heavy loads. Wheeled knuckleboom loaders are equipped with tires, offering mobility and agility to easily navigate different terrains. They are commonly used in construction and forestry applications. On the other hand, tracked knuckleboom loaders have metal tracks that provide better stability and weight distribution, making them suitable for challenging terrains like marshes or steep slopes. Tracked knuckleboom loaders are often used in logging operations and difficult construction sites. Both types offer efficient and precise load handling capabilities.</p></p>
