<p><p>Electrofishing is a technique used in fisheries management and research to sample fish populations in water bodies. <p><p>Electrofishing is a fishing technique that uses electricity to stun fish for population studies or harvest. There are three main market types of electrofishing equipment. Backpack models are portable and worn by a person wading in shallow waters. Tote barge models are larger and towed by an operator walking or driving along the shoreline. Boat-mounted models are designed for deeper waters and require a boat for operation. These different types cater to varying fishing environments and provide options for effective and efficient electrofishing operations.</p></p>
