<p><strong>The Automated DNA Sequencers Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Chain Terminator DNA Sequencing</li><li>Next Generation DNA Sequencing</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Automated DNA sequencers refer to advanced instruments that facilitate the sequencing of DNA molecules. Chain Terminator DNA Sequencing involves using fluorescently-labeled chain terminators to stop DNA replication at specific nucleotides, enabling the determination of DNA sequences. On the other hand, Next Generation DNA Sequencing utilizes various modern techniques like parallel sequencing and high-throughput methods to rapidly sequence multiple DNA fragments simultaneously. Both types of sequencers play a crucial role in genetic research, diagnostics, and personalized medicine by enabling efficient and accurate analysis of DNA sequences for various applications.</p></p>
