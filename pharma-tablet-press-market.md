<p><ul><li>Single Punch Tablet Press</li><li>Multiple Punch Tablet Press</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Pharma Tablet Press market is divided into two types - Single Punch Tablet Press and Multiple Punch Tablet Press. Single Punch Tablet Press is a type of tablet press where tablets are formed by a single compression force. It is mainly used for small-scale production and is suitable for making tablets of varying shapes and sizes. On the other hand, Multiple Punch Tablet Press is a more advanced type of tablet press where multiple punches compress the powder simultaneously, resulting in higher production rates. It is widely used in large-scale pharmaceutical manufacturing processes.</p></p>
