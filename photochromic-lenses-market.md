<p><p>Photochromic lenses, also known as transition lenses, are optical eyewear lenses that darken when exposed to ultraviolet (UV) light and return to their clear state when the UV light source is removed. The increasing prevalence of eye-related disorders and the growing awareness about eye protection are driving the demand for photochromic lenses. Eyewear manufacturers offer a range of customizable options such as lens color, shape, and size, catering to individual preferences and fashion trends. Additionally, the integration of photochromic technology in fashionable eyewear frames is gaining popularity among consumers, leading to the adoption of photochromic lenses as a fashion statement.</p><p>In conclusion, the photochromic lenses market is projected to grow at a CAGR of 10.2% during the forecast period.
