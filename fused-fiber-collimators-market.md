<p><p>The Fused Fiber Collimators market includes two main types: Single Mode Fiber Collimators and Multimode Fiber Collimators. Single Mode Fiber Collimators are designed to work with single mode fibers, which have a smaller core size and allow for long-distance transmission of light signals with low dispersion. On the other hand, Multimode Fiber Collimators are compatible with multimode fibers, which have a larger core size and enable the transmission of light signals over shorter distances. These collimators play a crucial role in the field of fiber optics by ensuring efficient and accurate signal transmission in their respective fiber types.</p></p>
