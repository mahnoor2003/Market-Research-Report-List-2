<p><p>Adhesive fibers are used in different industries such as textile, automotive, construction, and many others. There are various types of adhesive fibers available in the market: </p><p>1. Ordinary Viscose Fiber: These fibers are made from wood pulp and are most commonly used in the textile industry to produce fabrics like rayon. </p><p>2. Rich Fiber: This type of adhesive fiber is known for its high strength and durability. It is commonly used in the manufacture of high-performance textiles and industrial products. </p><p>3. Viscose Silk: These fibers are produced by treating cellulose with chemicals, resulting in a soft and lustrous material. They are widely used in the textile industry to create luxurious and elegant fabrics. </p><p>4. Viscose Strong Wire: These fibers have a high tensile strength and are commonly used in applications where strength and durability are required, such as reinforcing materials in composites or electrical wires.</p></p>
