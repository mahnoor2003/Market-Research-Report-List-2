<p><p>Medium voltage sensors are devices used to measure and monitor electrical parameters in medium voltage power systems. Voltage sensors measure the voltage levels, while current sensors measure the flowing current. PLC couplers are used to communicate between the sensors and the programmable logic controller (PLC). Combined sensors, on the other hand, can measure both voltage and current simultaneously. These sensors are essential in ensuring the safe and efficient operation of medium voltage power systems.</p></p>
