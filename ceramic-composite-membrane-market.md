<p><p>The Ceramic Composite Membrane market encompasses various types, including Flat-sheet Membrane and Pipe Membrane. Flat-sheet membranes refer to thin, planar structures that consist of a ceramic layer, often made of metal oxides or carbides, supported by a porous substrate. These membranes are commonly used in filtration processes due to their high permeability and excellent separation efficiency. On the other hand, Pipe Membranes are tubular structures with a ceramic coating inside, making them suitable for applications requiring high pressure and larger volumes of fluid filtration.</p></p>
