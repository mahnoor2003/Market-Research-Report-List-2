<p><p>Marine Subwoofers are designed specifically for use in marine environments. There are two main types: powered subwoofers and passive subwoofers. Powered subwoofers have built-in amplifiers, making them easier to install as they only require a power source. They provide a more convenient and compact solution. On the other hand, passive subwoofers require an external amplifier to be powered. They offer more flexibility as they can be matched with different amplifiers to achieve desired sound and power levels. Both types enhance the audio experience on boats or other marine vessels.</p></p>
