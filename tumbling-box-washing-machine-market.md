<p><p>The tumbling box washing machine market consists of two main types: fully automatic and semi-automatic machines. Fully automatic machines do not require any manual intervention throughout the entire washing process. Users simply need to load the clothes and select the desired settings for a hassle-free washing experience. On the other hand, semi-automatic machines require some manual intervention, such as transferring clothes from the washing to the drying tub. Although semi-automatic machines involve more effort, they are usually more affordable compared to fully automatic ones. Both types offer washing convenience, but vary in terms of user involvement and cost.</p></p>
