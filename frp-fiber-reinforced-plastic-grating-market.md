<p><strong>The FRP (Fiber-Reinforced Plastic) Grating Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Molded Grating</li><li>Pultruded Grating</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The FRP grating market is divided into two types: molded grating and pultruded grating. Molded grating is made by interweaving fiberglass strands and resin under high pressure to create a durable and lightweight product. It is commonly used in industries such as chemical processing, oil and gas, and power generation. On the other hand, pultruded grating is manufactured by pulling fiberglass strands through a resin bath and then shaping them into a grid pattern. It is known for its high strength and resistance to corrosion and is widely used in applications that require heavy load capacity.</p></p>
