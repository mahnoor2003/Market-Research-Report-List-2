<p><strong>The Embedded Pico Projectors Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>DLP (Digital Light Processing) Pico Projector</li><li>LCoS (Liquid Crystal on Silicon) Pico Projector</li><li>Laser-Beam-Steering (LBS) Pico Projector</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Embedded Pico Projectors are small projection devices that can be integrated into various electronic devices, such as smartphones, laptops, and cameras. The market for these projectors can be classified into three types: DLP (Digital Light Processing) Pico Projector, LCoS (Liquid Crystal on Silicon) Pico Projector, and Laser-Beam-Steering (LBS) Pico Projector. DLP Pico Projectors use micro mirrors to reflect light onto a screen, providing high image quality. LCoS Pico Projectors use liquid crystal panels to project images, offering better color reproduction. LBS Pico Projectors use laser beams to steer light, resulting in compact and energy-efficient devices.</p></p>
