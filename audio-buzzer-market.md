<p><p>The audio buzzer market comprises various types of buzzers. Piezo buzzers are commonly used, producing sound by creating vibrations in a piezoelectric material. Electromagnetic buzzers operate by generating sound through an electromagnet that attracts a metal diaphragm. Other types include magnetic buzzers, which utilize a magnet and coil to generate sound, and mechanical buzzers, which produce sound by the mechanical vibration of a diaphragm. These varieties offer different features and are employed in various applications, such as in alarms, timers, and communication devices.</p></p>
