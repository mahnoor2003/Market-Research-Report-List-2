<p><p>Document encryption software market consists of various encryption algorithms that help protect sensitive information. The IDEA algorithm, characterized by its simplicity and efficiency, is widely used for symmetric key encryption. RSA algorithm, based on the prime factorization problem, is commonly used for asymmetric key encryption. The AES algorithm, known for its high security and flexibility, is popular for symmetric key encryption. These algorithms ensure that documents are encrypted and can only be accessed with the corresponding decryption key, thus safeguarding important data and preserving privacy.</p></p>
