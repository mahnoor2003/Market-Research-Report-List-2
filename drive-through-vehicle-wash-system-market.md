<p><p>The drive-through vehicle wash system market is divided into two main types: touchless and friction. Touchless systems use high-pressure water jets and chemical cleaning agents to remove dirt and grime without physically touching the vehicle's surface. This method is popular because it reduces the risk of scratches and damage to the paintwork. On the other hand, friction systems use rotating brushes, cloth, or foam pads to physically scrub the vehicle's surface, providing a more thorough cleaning. Each type offers its own advantages, and customers can choose based on their preferences and needs.</p></p>
