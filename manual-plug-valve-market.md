<p><p>The manual plug valve market consists of three main types: two-way, three-way, and four-way plug valves. Two-way plug valves have two ports that control the flow of liquid or gas. Three-way plug valves have three ports and provide multiple flow options, such as diverting flow or merging two flows into one. Four-way plug valves have four ports and enable complex flow combinations, including the distribution of flow to different locations. These valves are manually operated and widely used in industries where precise control of flow is required.</p></p>
