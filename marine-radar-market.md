<p><p>The marine radar market is categorized into two types - X Band Radar and S Band Radar. X Band Radar operates in the frequency range of 8-12 GHz and is commonly used for short-range applications. It provides high resolution and accuracy and is ideal for navigation and collision avoidance purposes. On the other hand, S Band Radar operates in the frequency range of 2-4 GHz and is suitable for long-range radar applications. It offers better performance in adverse weather conditions and is often used for vessel traffic management and surveillance purposes.</p></p>
