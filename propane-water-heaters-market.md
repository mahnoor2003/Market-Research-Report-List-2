<p><p>Propane water heaters are categorized into two main market types: Tankless Propane Water Heaters and Tank Propane Water Heaters. Tankless Propane Water Heaters, as the name suggests, do not require a storage tank and heat water on-demand. They are efficient, save space, and provide a continuous supply of hot water. On the other hand, Tank Propane Water Heaters store and heat water in a tank, providing a ready supply of hot water. They are suitable for larger households and have a longer lifespan.</p></p>
