<p><ul><li>Lateral Flow</li><li>Agglutination Assays</li><li>Flow-Through</li><li>Biosensors</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Rapid medical diagnostic device market includes various types such as lateral flow assays, agglutination assays, flow-through systems, and biosensors. Lateral flow assays are simple and easy-to-use tests that detect the presence of specific analytes in a sample. Agglutination assays utilize the clumping of particles for detection. Flow-through systems pass the sample through a medium where reactions occur. Biosensors integrate biological components with transducers to detect and measure analytes. These devices offer quick and convenient diagnostic solutions for various medical conditions.</p></p>
