<p><p>Roll laminators are categorized into two types: heated roll laminators and cold roll laminators. Heated roll laminators use heat and pressure to apply adhesive laminating film onto materials, providing a secure and durable finish. They are commonly used for high-volume laminating applications and offer faster processing times. On the other hand, cold roll laminators do not require heat, as they utilize pressure-sensitive adhesive laminating films that are applied through pressure alone. They are typically used for heat-sensitive materials and delicate documents, offering a safer and more versatile laminating solution.</p></p>
