<p><p>orthopaedic shoes are designed to provide support and correct any biomechanical issues in the feet. There are various types available in the market. Flatfoot orthopaedic shoes are specifically designed for individuals with flat arches, providing arch support and stability. Cavus orthopaedic shoes are made for people with high arches, providing cushioning and shock absorption. Calcaneal spur orthopaedic shoes have additional padding to alleviate pain caused by heel spurs. Varus orthopaedic shoes help correct inward tilting of the ankle, while valgus orthopaedic shoes help correct outward tilting. Other types of orthopaedic shoes cater to specific foot conditions such as bunions or diabetic foot.</p></p>
