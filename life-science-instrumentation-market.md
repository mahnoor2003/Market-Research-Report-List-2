Flow Cytometry enables the analysis of cells and particles based on their physical and chemical characteristics. NGS (Next-Generation Sequencing) facilitates the rapid sequencing of DNA or RNA molecules. PCR (Polymerase Chain Reaction) amplifies DNA sequences for analysis. Lastly, Microscopy allows for the observation and examination of tiny biological structures.</p></p>
