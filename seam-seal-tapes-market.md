<p><p>Seam seal tapes are used in various industries to provide waterproofing and air leakage resistance in products such as garments, footwear, and tents. There are two main types of seam seal tapes: 2-layer and 3-layer. The 2-layer tapes consist of a base layer and an adhesive layer, which are heat sealed onto the fabric. On the other hand, 3-layer tapes have an additional backing layer, offering enhanced strength and durability. These tapes are designed to securely bond the layers together, ensuring effective sealing and superior performance of the end product.</p></p>
