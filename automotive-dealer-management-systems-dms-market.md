The DMS market can be categorized into cloud-based and on-premise systems. Cloud-based DMS is a web-based solution hosted on remote servers that allows users to access and manage data online. On the other hand, on-premise DMS is installed locally on dealership computers. While cloud-based DMS offers scalability, flexibility, and remote access, on-premise DMS provides more control, security, and customization options. Both types aim to streamline processes and enhance productivity in automotive dealership management.</p></p>
