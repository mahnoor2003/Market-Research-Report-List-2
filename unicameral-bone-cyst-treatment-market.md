<p><p>Unicameral bone cyst (UBC) is a benign bone lesion that commonly occurs in children and adolescents. The treatment for UBC aims to alleviate pain, prevent pathological fractures, and promote bone healing. The primary treatment options include non-surgical methods such as observation, steroid injections, or minimally invasive procedures like cyst decompression and bone grafting. In more severe cases, surgery may be required to stabilize the bone, filling the cyst with bone grafts or synthetic materials, or performing curettage and bone grafting procedures.</p><p>The Unicameral Bone Cyst Treatment Market is projected to witness significant growth during the forecast period.
