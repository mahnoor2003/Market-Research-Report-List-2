<p><p>Feed mycotoxin detoxifiers refer to substances or compounds used to reduce the harmful effects of mycotoxins in animal feed. There are primarily two types of detoxifiers available in the market: mycotoxin binders and mycotoxin modifiers. Mycotoxin binders bind to mycotoxins, preventing them from being absorbed by animals, while mycotoxin modifiers work by altering the structure or toxicity of mycotoxins. Both types of detoxifiers are important in ensuring the safety and quality of animal feed and ultimately protecting animal health.</p></p>
