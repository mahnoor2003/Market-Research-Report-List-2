<p><p>The Portable Neutron Detector Market comprises two types: the Gas Proportional Detector and the Scintillation Neutron Detector. Gas Proportional Detectors utilize a gas-filled chamber that detects and measures neutrons using the principle of ionization. These detectors offer high sensitivity and accuracy, making them suitable for various applications such as nuclear power plants and research facilities. On the other hand, Scintillation Neutron Detectors use a scintillator material that emits light when struck by neutrons. These detectors are commonly used in applications such as border security, environmental monitoring, and oil exploration due to their fast response and energy resolution capabilities.</p></p>
