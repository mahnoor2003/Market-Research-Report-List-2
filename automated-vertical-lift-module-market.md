<p><p>Automated Vertical Lift Modules (VLMs) are storage systems that consist of vertically arranged trays or shelves within a tall enclosure. Single Level Delivery VLMs allow access to only one tray at a time, enabling operators to retrieve or store items placed within that tray. On the other hand, Dual Level Delivery VLMs offer access to two trays simultaneously, increasing the storage capacity and allowing for more efficient picking and stocking processes. These different market types cater to various storage needs and operational requirements of businesses.</p></p>
