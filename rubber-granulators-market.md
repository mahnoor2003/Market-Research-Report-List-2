<p><p>Rubber granulators market types consist of single seal, double seals, and tandem mechanical seals. Single seal granulators use a single seal to prevent leakage of the granulating material. Double seal granulators employ two seals for enhanced protection against leakage. Tandem mechanical seal granulators have two sets of seals stacked together to ensure a high level of containment and prevent any escape of the granulated rubber. These different types of seals cater to various customer needs and provide varying levels of security in the rubber granulator market.</p></p>
