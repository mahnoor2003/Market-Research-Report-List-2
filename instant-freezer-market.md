<p><p>The Instant Freezer market is divided into three major types: Push Type, Screw Type, and Elevating Type. Push Type Instant Freezers are designed with a push plate that instantly freezes food items by pushing them against the freezing surface. Screw Type Instant Freezers use rotating screws to transport food items through the freezing chamber to achieve rapid freezing. Elevating Type Instant Freezers utilize an elevating conveyor system to move food items vertically through the freezing chamber, ensuring efficient and quick freezing. These different types cater to diverse freezing needs in the market.</p></p>
