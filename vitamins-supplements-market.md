<p><p>There are two main types of vitamin and supplement markets: natural vitamins and synthetic vitamins. Natural vitamins are derived from whole food sources and are considered more bioavailable and easily absorbed by the body. They are often perceived as more organic and sustainable. Synthetic vitamins, on the other hand, are chemically produced and often cheaper to manufacture. While they may still provide essential nutrients, their efficacy and absorption rates can be lower compared to natural vitamins. Both markets cater to individuals seeking to supplement their diet with additional nutrients.</p></p>
