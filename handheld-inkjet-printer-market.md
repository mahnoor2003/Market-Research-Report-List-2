<p><p>Handheld inkjet printers are available in two market types: USB and photoelectric interface. The USB market type refers to printers that can be directly connected to a computer or other devices via a USB cable, allowing for convenient printing and control. On the other hand, the photoelectric interface market type includes printers that utilize sensors to detect and print on various surfaces. This allows for greater flexibility in printing on objects such as curved or irregular surfaces, making it suitable for industries that require specialized printing requirements.</p></p>
