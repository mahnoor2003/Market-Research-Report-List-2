<p><p>The Transient Voltage Suppressor (TVS) Diodes Market includes two main types: Uni-polar TVS and Bi-polar TVS. Uni-polar TVS diodes are designed to only conduct in one direction, which means they can protect against either positive or negative voltage spikes. On the other hand, Bi-polar TVS diodes can conduct in both directions and offer protection against both positive and negative voltage spikes. These diodes are used in various applications to protect electronic components from transient overvoltage events and ensure the smooth functioning of electronic systems.</p></p>
