<p><p>The mobile phone chargers market consist of two main types: wired chargers and wireless chargers. Wired chargers are the traditional method of charging, requiring a physical cable connection between the charger and the phone. They are widely available and typically less expensive than wireless chargers. On the other hand, wireless chargers use electromagnetic fields to transfer energy from the charger to the phone without the need for cables. Although they offer convenience and eliminate cable clutter, wireless chargers are generally slower and more expensive compared to wired chargers.</p></p>
