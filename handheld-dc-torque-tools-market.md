<p><p>Handheld DC torque tools are portable devices used to measure and apply torque (rotational force) to fasteners such as nuts, bolts, and screws. This growth can be attributed to several factors, including the increasing demand for precise torque applications, the growing automotive industry, and the rising adoption of electric power tools.</p><p>One of the key driving factors for the market is the increasing need for accurate torque applications. Handheld DC torque tools offer high accuracy and precision, ensuring that fasteners are tightened to the required specifications. This is particularly important in critical applications where overtightening or undertightening can have severe consequences.</p><p>Additionally, the automotive industry is a significant end-user of handheld DC torque tools. With the increasing production of vehicles worldwide, the demand for torque tools is expected to grow substantially. Moreover, the growing popularity of electric power tools, which offer enhanced efficiency and ease of use compared to pneumatic and hydraulic torque tools, is further propelling the market growth.</p><p>Furthermore, the market is witnessing the emergence of advanced features in handheld DC torque tools, such as digital displays, wireless connectivity, and data logging capabilities.
