<p><p>The biosensors for point-of-care diagnostics market consists of various types of sensors used for quick and accurate medical tests. Electrochemical biosensors measure the electrical signals produced by chemical reactions. Thermal biosensors detect temperature changes caused by specific reactions. Piezoelectric biosensors use crystals that generate an electrical charge when subjected to pressure. Accelerometer biosensors measure acceleration and movement. Optical biosensors utilize light to detect and analyze biological components. Together, these biosensors enable rapid and reliable diagnostic measurements at the point of care, improving healthcare outcomes.</p></p>
