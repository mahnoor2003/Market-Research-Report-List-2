Diaphragm pumps utilize a flexible diaphragm to facilitate the movement of water, while plunger pumps use reciprocating plungers to create pressure. Other types may include piston pumps, rotary pumps, or centrifugal pumps. These pumps are designed to handle high-pressure water applications, such as cleaning, irrigation, firefighting, or power washing. Each type offers unique features and benefits, catering to different industrial or residential needs.</p></p>
