<p><p>The cold-climate heat pump market consists of two main types: ductless heat pumps and central-ducted heat pumps. Ductless heat pumps are compact units that can be easily installed in individual rooms or areas, providing targeted heating. They are energy-efficient and offer flexibility in terms of zonal heating. On the other hand, central-ducted heat pumps use a network of ducts to distribute heat throughout the entire building. They are suitable for larger spaces and provide uniform heating. Both types of heat pumps are designed to operate efficiently in cold climates, ensuring reliable heating even in extreme weather conditions.</p></p>
