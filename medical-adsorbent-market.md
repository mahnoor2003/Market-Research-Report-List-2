<p><p>The Medical Adsorbent Market encompasses three main types: Synthetic Resin, Activated Carbon, and Polysaccharide. Synthetic Resin adsorbents are chemically engineered materials that effectively remove harmful substances from the body. Activated Carbon adsorbents have a highly porous structure that allows them to trap and eliminate toxins. Polysaccharide adsorbents, on the other hand, are derived from natural substances and are known for their biocompatibility and low toxicity. All three types play a crucial role in medical applications by providing effective adsorption and purification of harmful substances from various bodily fluids.</p></p>
