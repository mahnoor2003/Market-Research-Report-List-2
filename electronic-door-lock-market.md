<p><p>An electronic door lock is a security device that functions using electrical or digital signals, eliminating the need for traditional mechanical keys. Factors contributing to this growth include the increasing focus on security and safety concerns, technological advancements, rising adoption of home automation systems, and the convenience offered by electronic door locks.</p><p>The market is witnessing a shift from traditional mechanical locks to electronic solutions due to their enhanced security features and ease of use. These locks provide keyless access, eliminating the risk of lost or stolen keys. Additionally, electronic door locks offer advanced features like remote access control, integration with smart home systems, and real-time monitoring, further boosting their demand.</p><p>Another major trend in the electronic door lock market is the integration of biometric technology, such as fingerprint or facial recognition, for enhanced security and convenience.
