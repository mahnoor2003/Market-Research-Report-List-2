<p><strong>The Infrared Imaging Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Cooled Infrared Imaging</li><li>Uncooled Infrared Imaging</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Infrared Imaging market consists of two primary types: Cooled Infrared Imaging and Uncooled Infrared Imaging. Cooled Infrared Imaging involves the use of a cooling mechanism to maintain the detector temperature, which improves the accuracy and sensitivity of the imaging system. This type is typically used for high-end applications that require detailed thermal imaging. On the other hand, Uncooled Infrared Imaging does not require any cooling mechanism, as it utilizes microbolometers that work at normal room temperature. This type is cost-effective and commonly used in applications such as surveillance systems and thermal cameras.</p></p>
