<p><p>The data integration app market can be categorized into three types: native apps, hybrid apps, and web apps. Native apps are specifically built for a particular platform or operating system, offering better performance and user experience. Hybrid apps combine the advantages of native and web apps, using web technologies within a native wrapper. They offer cross-platform compatibility and access to device features. Web apps, on the other hand, are accessed via web browsers and do not require installation. They offer flexibility and easy updates but may have limitations in terms of performance and offline capabilities.</p></p>
