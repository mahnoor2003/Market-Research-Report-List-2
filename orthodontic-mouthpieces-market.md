<p><p>Orthodontic mouthpieces can be classified into two types: transparent and opaque. Transparent mouthpieces are made from clear materials such as plastic or ceramic, allowing for a discreet and inconspicuous appearance when worn. They blend well with the natural color of the teeth, making them less noticeable. On the other hand, opaque mouthpieces are made from colored or metal materials, which tend to be more visible in the mouth. They are often chosen for aesthetic reasons, as they can be customized with different colors or patterns to make a fashion statement.</p></p>
