<p><p>The trauma implantable consumables market includes several types of medical devices used in the treatment of fractures and trauma. These include intramedullary nails, hollow nails, ordinary nail boards, locking pin plates, external fixators, as well as pins and cables. Intramedullary nails are inserted within the bone to provide stability, while hollow nails are used in cases with limited blood supply. Ordinary nail boards are typically used for simple fractures, and locking pin plates offer enhanced stability. External fixators stabilize fractures externally, and pins and cables assist in the alignment and fixation of bones.</p></p>
