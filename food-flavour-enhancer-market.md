<p><p>The food flavor enhancer market encompasses various types such as Monosodium Glutamate (MSG), Hydrolyzed Vegetable Protein (HVP), Yeast Extract, and Others. MSG is a commonly used flavor enhancer that enhances the taste of food. HVP is a protein derived from vegetables that imparts a meat-like flavor to vegetarian and vegan food products. Yeast extract is a natural alternative to MSG, providing a savory taste. The "Others" category includes various flavor enhancers like disodium inosinate, disodium guanylate, and nucleotides, which are used to enhance specific flavors in food products.</p></p>
