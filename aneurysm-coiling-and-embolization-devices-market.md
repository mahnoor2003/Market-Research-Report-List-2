<p><p>Aneurysm coiling and embolization devices are medical devices used in the treatment of brain aneurysms. A brain aneurysm is a weak or bulging spot in the wall of an artery in the brain that can rupture and cause bleeding into the surrounding tissues, leading to severe complications or even death. Aneurysm coiling and embolization devices are used to prevent this rupture by blocking the blood flow to the aneurysm and promoting blood clot formation.</p><p>The global market for aneurysm coiling and embolization devices is expected to witness significant growth in the coming years. <p><p>Aneurysm coiling and embolization devices are used in the treatment of brain aneurysms. Embolization coils are introduced into the aneurysm to trigger blood clotting and prevent rupture. Balloon and stent-assisted coils help in stabilizing the coils within the aneurysm. Access and delivery devices aid in reaching the precise location of the aneurysm during the procedure. Aneurysm clips are alternative devices used to clip the neck of the aneurysm, stopping blood flow. Flow diversion devices redirect blood flow and promote healing within the aneurysm.</p></p>
