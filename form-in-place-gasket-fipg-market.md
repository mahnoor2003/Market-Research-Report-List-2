<p><p>Form in Place Gasket (FIPG) market types include Liquid Foam Gaskets, Thixotropic Foam Gaskets, and Compact Gaskets. Liquid Foam Gaskets are applied in their liquid form and expand into foam when exposed to air, providing a flexible and uniform seal. Thixotropic Foam Gaskets also start as a liquid but thicken upon application, offering increased stability and preventing sagging. Compact Gaskets, on the other hand, are preformed gaskets that are placed directly onto the surface, providing a reliable and efficient seal. These different market types cater to various sealing needs in industries such as automotive, electronics, and industrial manufacturing.</p></p>
