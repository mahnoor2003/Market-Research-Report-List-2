<p><strong>The Expedition Motor Yachts Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Displacement Hull</li><li>Semi-Displacement Hull</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Expedition Motor Yachts market comprises two main types of hull designs: Displacement Hull and Semi-Displacement Hull. A Displacement Hull is known for its efficiency and stability, as it moves through water by displacing the amount of water equal to its weight. This type is popular among long-range cruisers due to its fuel economy. On the other hand, a Semi-Displacement Hull offers a combination of displacement and planing characteristics. It allows the yacht to achieve higher speeds while maintaining some fuel efficiency, making it suitable for both cruising and higher-speed applications.</p></p>
