<p><p>The capsule and tablet printing ink market can be categorized into two types based on the ink composition: solvent-based and water-based. Solvent-based inks are made of organic solvents that help dissolve the ink components, enhancing its durability and resistance. These inks are commonly used in industries where fast-drying and smudge-resistant prints are required. On the other hand, water-based inks are composed of water as the main solvent, making them more environmentally friendly. They are commonly used in industries that prioritize sustainability and have less strict requirements for print resilience.</p></p>
