<p><p>The electronic fragrance diffuser market comprises three main types: Ultrasonic diffusers, which use high-frequency vibrations to break down essential oils into a fine mist that is released into the air; Evaporation diffusers, which rely on air currents to evaporate essential oils from a pad or reservoir; and Electrothermal diffusers, which heat the essential oils to release their fragrance. These diffusers cater to different preferences and requirements, offering consumers various options to disperse pleasing scents in their homes or other spaces.</p></p>
