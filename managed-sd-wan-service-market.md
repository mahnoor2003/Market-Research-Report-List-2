<p><p>Managed SD-WAN service market includes two major types: cloud-based and on-premises. Cloud-based SD-WAN solutions utilize cloud infrastructure to manage and optimize wide area networks. They offer ease of deployment, flexibility, and scalability while reducing hardware requirements. On the other hand, on-premises SD-WAN is deployed within an organization's own infrastructure, offering greater control and security. It requires dedicated hardware and software, providing organizations with complete ownership and control over their network. Both types cater to different needs, with cloud-based focusing on simplicity and scalability, and on-premises prioritizing security and control.</p></p>
