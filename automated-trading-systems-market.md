<p><p>Automated Trading Systems (ATS) are used by financial institutions to execute trades in an automated manner. There are two main market types for ATS: On-Premise and Cloud-Based. On-Premise ATS is installed locally on the organization's infrastructure, offering complete control and customization options, but requires significant upfront investment. Conversely, Cloud-Based ATS is hosted on remote servers accessible via the internet, providing flexibility, scalability, and cost-effectiveness, as the system is managed by a third-party provider. Both market types have their own advantages and considerations, which organizations analyze to choose the appropriate ATS solution that suits their needs.</p></p>
