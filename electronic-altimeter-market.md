<p><p>The electronic altimeter market can be divided into two main types: on-board altimeters and altimeters for other markets. On-board altimeters are primarily used in aircraft and spacecraft to measure altitude and provide information to pilots. They are designed to withstand the harsh conditions and high altitudes associated with aviation. On the other hand, altimeters for other markets refer to altimeters used in various applications outside of aviation, such as hiking, mountaineering, and sports. These altimeters are typically lightweight, portable, and offer features like weather forecasting and GPS integration.</p></p>
