<p><p>The Millimeter Wave Body Scanner market encompasses two main types: Flat Scanning and Cylindrical Scanning. Flat Scanning utilizes stationary panels or antennas placed opposite each other to create an imaging field. It is commonly used in airports for whole-body scanning. On the other hand, Cylindrical Scanning employs rotating arrays or antennas to generate a 360-degree imaging field. This type is often used in security applications where 3D images of a subject are required, such as in high-security facilities or border control.</p></p>
