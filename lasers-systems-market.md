<p><strong>The Lasers Systems Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Continuous Lasers Systems</li><li>Pulsed Lasers Systems</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Laser systems are categorized into two main types: continuous lasers systems and pulsed lasers systems. Continuous laser systems emit a continuous beam of laser light and are commonly used in applications such as cutting, welding, and engraving. Pulsed laser systems, on the other hand, emit short bursts of laser light and are suitable for tasks like laser ablation, marking, and material processing. These systems find applications in various industries including healthcare, communications, military, and research. Each type of laser system has its own set of advantages and applications based on the specific requirements of the task at hand.</p></p>
