<p><ul><li>Inline AOI Equipment</li><li>Offline AOI Equipment</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Automatic Optical Inspection (AOI) market can be categorized into two types: Inline AOI Equipment and Offline AOI Equipment. Inline AOI Equipment refers to inspection systems integrated into the production line, ensuring real-time inspection for defects and abnormalities. It enables immediate identification and rectification, minimizing production disruptions. On the other hand, Offline AOI Equipment conducts inspections after the manufacturing process, examining finished products for quality assurance. While it may not prevent immediate production issues, it helps maintain quality standards and identify any defects before delivery.</p></p>
