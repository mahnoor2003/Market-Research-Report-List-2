<p><p>Electronic pressure regulators are devices used to accurately control and maintain pressure levels in various applications. There are two main types of electronic pressure regulators: single-stage and multi-stage. </p><p>Single-stage electronic pressure regulators have a single control valve that adjusts pressure according to the setpoint. They are commonly used in applications where a fixed pressure is required.</p><p>Multi-stage electronic pressure regulators have multiple control valves that allow for more precise and accurate pressure regulation. They are used in applications that require fine-tuning and control of pressure levels, such as in advanced industrial processes or scientific experiments.</p></p>
