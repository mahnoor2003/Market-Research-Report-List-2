<p><p>The Structural Health Monitoring (SHM) Systems Market encompasses two types of systems: wired and wireless. Wired Structural Health Monitoring Systems are characterized by physical connections between sensors and data acquisition devices, allowing real-time monitoring of structural health conditions. On the other hand, Wireless Structural Health Monitoring Systems employ wireless communication technologies to transmit data from sensors to the data acquisition devices, providing flexibility in installation and reducing the need for extensive wiring. Both systems aim to monitor and assess the structural integrity of various infrastructures and ensure timely maintenance and repairs.</p></p>
