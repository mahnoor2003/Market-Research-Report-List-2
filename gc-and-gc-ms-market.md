<p><p>Gas Chromatography (GC) is a technique used in analytical chemistry to separate and identify compounds in a mixture. GC systems are used in various industries such as pharmaceuticals, environmental analysis, and food and beverage testing.</p><p>Gas Chromatography-Mass Spectrometry (GC-MS) is an advanced variant of GC that combines gas chromatography with mass spectrometry. This technique allows for the identification and quantification of compounds present in a sample by separating and analyzing their mass-to-charge ratios. GC-MS systems are widely employed in forensic analysis, drug detection, and environmental monitoring.</p></p>
