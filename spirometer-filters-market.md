<p><p>Spirometer filters are used to ensure the accuracy and reliability of spirometry tests. There are two main types of spirometer filters available in the market: fluid filters and air filters. Fluid filters are specifically designed to prevent the contamination of the device when conducting spirometry tests using liquids, such as medications or saline solutions. On the other hand, air filters are used to filter out any particles or contaminants from ambient air during spirometry testing. Both types of filters play a crucial role in maintaining the precision and cleanliness of spirometry equipment.</p></p>
