<p><p>The temperature and humidity logger market consists of two main types: internal loggers and external loggers. Internal loggers are designed to be placed within the monitored environment, such as inside a room or storage facility. They are compact and often wireless, providing real-time data on temperature and humidity levels. External loggers, on the other hand, are placed outside the controlled environment and can withstand harsh conditions. They are commonly used for outdoor applications or remote monitoring. Both types serve different purposes and cater to specific monitoring needs in various industries.</p></p>
