<p><p>The Electric Torque Wrench market consists of two main types: Straight Type and Angled Type. Straight Type Electric Torque Wrenches are designed with a straight handle, allowing for a direct and precise application of torque. They are suitable for tasks that require a straight-on approach, such as tightening bolts in a linear manner. On the other hand, Angled Type Electric Torque Wrenches have a bent or angled handle, allowing for easier access to tight or confined spaces. They are ideal for tasks that require torque in hard-to-reach areas or at angles.</p></p>
