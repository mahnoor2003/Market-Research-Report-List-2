<p><p>The Data Transmission Module Market is composed of two main types: Wired Modules and Wireless Modules. Wired Modules rely on physical connections, such as cables or wires, to transmit data between devices. They are known for their reliability and stability. On the other hand, Wireless Modules use wireless technology, such as Wi-Fi or Bluetooth, to transmit data without the need for physical connections. They offer greater flexibility and mobility but may be more susceptible to interference and signal loss. Both types have their advantages and are used in various applications based on the specific requirements.</p></p>
