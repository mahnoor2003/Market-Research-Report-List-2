<p><p>Pipetting systems market is categorized into three types - automatic, semiautomatic, and manual. Automatic systems simplify the pipetting process by precisely aspirating and dispensing liquid samples with minimal user intervention. Semiautomatic systems require some manual involvement, such as loading the sample and initiating the pipetting action, but the system performs the pipetting automatically. Manual systems involve complete manual operation by the user and require careful handling to ensure accuracy. Each system type offers different levels of automation and user control in the pipetting process to cater to various laboratory needs.</p></p>
