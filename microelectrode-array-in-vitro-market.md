<p><p>The microelectrode array in vitro market consists of three main types: classical MEA, multiwell-MEA, and CMOS-MEA. Classical MEA includes traditional MEA chips used for neuronal and cardiac cell studies. Multiwell-MEA involves multiple MEA chips arranged in a multiwell format, enabling higher throughput testing and data collection. CMOS-MEA utilizes complementary metal-oxide-semiconductor technology, allowing for higher density and integration of microelectrodes on a single chip. These different types cater to various research needs, providing researchers with options for in vitro testing and analysis.</p></p>
