<p><strong>The Die Cutting Machine Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Metal-to-Metal</li><li>Perfing</li><li>Slitting</li><li>Kiss Cutting</li><li>Scoring</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Die cutting machines are available in various types to cater to different cutting needs. Metal-to-metal die cutting machines are used for cutting metal sheets. Perfing machines are designed for perforation tasks. Slitting machines are used for cutting materials into strips. Kiss cutting machines are used for cutting thin materials without cutting through the backing material. Scoring machines are used to create creases in materials. Each type of die cutting machine serves a specific purpose in the market based on the cutting requirements of the users.</p></p>
