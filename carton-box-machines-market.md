<p><p>The carton box machines market can be divided into two main types: semi-automatic and fully-automatic. Semi-automatic machines require some human intervention during the packaging process, such as manually feeding the carton blanks or sealing the boxes. On the other hand, fully-automatic machines can operate without human involvement, performing tasks like feeding, folding, gluing, and sealing the carton boxes automatically. Both types of machines offer various advantages, depending on the production needs and level of automation desired by the packaging industry.</p></p>
