LV switchgear is commonly used for power distribution, motor control, and electrical fault protection.</p><p>MV Switchgear: MV switchgear stands for Medium Voltage switchgear, which is designed to operate at voltages between 1,000 and 35,000 volts. It includes various equipment and devices such as circuit breakers, load break switches, and transformers to control, protect, and distribute electrical power in industrial or commercial applications. MV switchgear is commonly used in substations, power plants, and large-scale industrial facilities to ensure safe and reliable power distribution.</p></p>
