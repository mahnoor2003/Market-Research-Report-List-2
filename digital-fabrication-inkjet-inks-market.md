<p><strong>The Digital Fabrication Inkjet Inks Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Digital Fabrication Inkjet Inks Based on Dye</li><li>Digital Fabrication Inkjet Inks Based on Pigment</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Digital fabrication inkjet inks are categorized based on the type of colorants used, either dye or pigment. Dye-based inks are made up of colored substances that are dissolved in a liquid solvent. These inks are known for providing bright and vibrant colors but have limited durability and are susceptible to fading over time. On the other hand, pigment-based inks consist of solid particles that are dispersed in a liquid medium. These inks offer better color stability and longevity as the solid particles provide better UV resistance and resistance to environmental factors.</p></p>
