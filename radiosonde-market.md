<p><p>Radiosondes are meteorological instruments used for weather observations. The radiosonde market can be broadly classified into three types based on their range capabilities: short-range, mid-range, and long-range. Short-range radiosondes are designed for atmospheric monitoring within a limited distance, typically within a few kilometers. Mid-range radiosondes have a wider reach, allowing for weather data collection up to hundreds of kilometers. Long-range radiosondes, on the other hand, have the farthest range capabilities, enabling weather monitoring over vast distances, including transoceanic flights and remote locations.</p></p>
