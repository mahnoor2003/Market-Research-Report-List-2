<p><strong>The Two-stage Electro-hydraulic Servo Valve Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Nozzle Flapper Valve</li><li>Jet Action Valve</li><li>Direct Drive Valve</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The two-stage electro-hydraulic servo valve market includes three types of valves: nozzle flapper valve, jet action valve, and direct drive valve. A nozzle flapper valve uses a nozzle to generate a high-velocity jet which deflects a flapper, controlling fluid flow. A jet action valve employs a jet to move a spool and regulate flow. In contrast, a direct drive valve directly moves the spool to control fluid flow. These valves are essential in various industries, allowing precise control of hydraulic systems for improved performance and efficiency.</p></p>
