Panel Installation: These relays are installed directly onto the generator control panel, providing a compact and easily accessible protection solution suitable for smaller generators or in locations with limited space.</p><p>3. Embedded relays are typically designed for larger generators and provide advanced features tailored to specific generator models and manufacturers.</p></p>
