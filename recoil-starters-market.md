<p><p>Recoil starters, also known as pull-starters or rewind starters, are mechanical devices used in small engines to manually start their operation. These starters are commonly found in lawnmowers, chainsaws, generators, and other outdoor equipment. Recoil starters feature a rope that is pulled out and then quickly released, causing the engine's flywheel to rotate and initiate the ignition process.</p><p>The current outlook of the recoil starters market is positive and expected to witness steady growth in the coming years.
