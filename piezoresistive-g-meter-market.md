<p><p>The Piezoresistive G Meter market consists of three types: 1 Axis, 2 Axis, and 3 Axis. The 1 Axis G Meter measures acceleration along a single axis, providing data on motion in one direction only. The 2 Axis G Meter, on the other hand, measures acceleration along two axes, allowing for analysis of motion in two different directions. Lastly, the 3 Axis G Meter measures acceleration on all three axes, providing a comprehensive view of motion in three different directions. These different types of G Meters enable users to accurately measure and analyze various forms of motion depending on their specific needs.</p></p>
