<p><p>High-speed train bogies are an essential component of train systems that enable efficient and smooth operation at high speeds. There are two main types: H-form and O-form bogies. H-form bogies are designed with two wheelsets connected by a common frame, providing stability and reducing vibrations. O-form bogies have a single wheelset supported by an independent frame, allowing for better maneuverability and improved passenger comfort. Both types have their advantages and applications, and the market for high-speed train bogies comprises these two distinct market segments.</p></p>
