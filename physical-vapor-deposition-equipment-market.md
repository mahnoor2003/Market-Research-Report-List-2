<p><strong>The Physical Vapor Deposition Equipment Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Cathodic Arc Vapor Deposition</li><li>Electron Beam Vapor Deposition</li><li>Sputter Deposition</li><li>Others</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Physical Vapor Deposition Equipment Market comprises various types, including Cathodic Arc Vapor Deposition, Electron Beam Vapor Deposition, Sputter Deposition, and Others. Cathodic Arc Vapor Deposition involves using a high-current, low-voltage electric arc to create a plasma that deposits metal ions onto the substrate. Electron Beam Vapor Deposition utilizes an electron beam to vaporize the coating material and deposit it onto the substrate. Sputter Deposition involves bombarding a target with high-energy ions, causing the atoms to be ejected and deposited as a thin film onto the substrate. Other methods include evaporation and pulsed laser deposition.</p></p>
