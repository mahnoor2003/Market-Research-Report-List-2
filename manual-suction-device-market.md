It consists of a manually operated pump mechanism connected to a collection canister and tubing, allowing healthcare professionals to provide immediate suction as needed.</p><p>The manual suction device market is expected to witness significant growth during the forecast period. Factors contributing to this growth include the increasing demand for portable and cost-effective medical devices, the rising prevalence of chronic respiratory disorders and emergency situations, and the need for suctioning in ambulatory settings. Additionally, the manual suction device market is driven by the growing geriatric population and the rising number of surgeries and trauma cases globally.</p><p>Moreover, the market is witnessing several trends that are shaping its growth. One such trend is the technological advancements in manual suction devices, leading to improved efficiency and ease of use. The COVID-19 pandemic has also contributed to the market growth, with a surge in demand for manual suction devices in hospitals and healthcare facilities to tackle the respiratory symptoms associated with the virus.</p><p>In conclusion, the manual suction device market is poised for substantial growth due to the increasing demand for portable and cost-effective medical devices, the rising prevalence of respiratory disorders, and the need for suctioning in various healthcare settings.
