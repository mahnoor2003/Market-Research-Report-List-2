<p>&nbsp;</p>
<p><p>Perovskite solar cell market types can be divided into two categories: normal structure and inverted structure. In the normal structure, the perovskite layer is sandwiched between the electron and hole transport layers, allowing efficient charge extraction. On the other hand, the inverted structure has a reversed layer order, which facilitates better charge collection and reduced hysteresis in voltage-current characteristics. Both market types have their advantages and disadvantages, and their usage largely depends on specific application requirements and manufacturing processes.</p></p>
