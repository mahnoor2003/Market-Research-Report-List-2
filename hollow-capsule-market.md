<p><p>A hollow capsule is a pharmaceutical capsule that is composed of two halves, which fit together to encapsulate powdered or liquid medications. Hollow capsules are commonly used in the pharmaceutical industry as a convenient and reliable method of dosage delivery.</p><p>The global hollow capsule market is expected to experience steady growth in the coming years. Factors driving the market growth include increasing consumer preference for capsules over tablets due to ease of swallowing, rising demand for dietary supplements and nutraceuticals, and advancements in capsule manufacturing technologies.
