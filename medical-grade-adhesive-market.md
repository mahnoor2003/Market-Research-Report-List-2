<p><p>Medical grade adhesive refers to a type of adhesive that is specifically formulated and manufactured to be safe for use on human skin or inside the body. The increasing demand for medical devices, advancements in healthcare infrastructure, and a growing aging population are major drivers of market growth. Additionally, the rising incidence of chronic diseases and the need for more efficient and cost-effective medical treatments have also contributed to the increasing adoption of medical grade adhesives.</p><p>Moreover, the market is witnessing the emergence of several trends that are further driving its growth.
