<p><strong>The Remote Afterloading System Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>HDR Afterloaders</li><li>PDR Afterloaders</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The remote afterloading system market consists of two main types: HDR afterloaders and PDR afterloaders. HDR (High-Dose Rate) afterloaders are devices used in brachytherapy, a type of cancer treatment, to deliver a high dose of radiation to the tumor site. PDR (Pulse-Dose Rate) afterloaders, on the other hand, deliver radiation pulses at a regular interval. Both systems are remotely controlled and enable precise and targeted radiation therapy, ensuring better patient outcomes and minimizing radiation exposure to healthy tissues.</p></p>
