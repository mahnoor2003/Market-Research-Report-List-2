<p><p>Antidepressant drugs are classified into various types. Tricyclic antidepressants work by increasing the levels of specific brain chemicals. Serotonin-norepinephrine inhibitors increase the levels of serotonin and norepinephrine in the brain. Atypical antipsychotics can also be used as antidepressants. Selective serotonin reuptake inhibitors increase serotonin levels. Benzodiazepines are often prescribed for anxiety and can help with depression symptoms. Tetracyclic antidepressants work by affecting various brain chemicals. Monoamine oxidase inhibitors increase the levels of certain chemicals in the brain. Finally, there are other types of antidepressants available on the market as well.</p></p>
