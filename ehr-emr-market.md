<p><p>The EHR-EMR market includes two main types: on-premise and cloud-based. On-premise solutions involve installing and maintaining the EHR-EMR systems within a healthcare facility's infrastructure, allowing for better control and data security. In contrast, cloud-based solutions rely on external servers, offering flexibility, scalability, and easier access from multiple devices. These cloud-based systems are easily maintained and updated by the vendor, reducing the burden on healthcare providers.
