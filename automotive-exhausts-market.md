<p><p>The automotive exhausts market is composed of various types of technology used to reduce emissions from vehicles. Diesel Oxidation Catalyst (DOC) is used to convert harmful gases into less harmful substances. Diesel Particulate Filter (DPF) traps and removes particulate matter from diesel exhaust. Lean Nox Trap (LNT) reduces nitrogen oxide emissions from lean-burning engines. Selective Catalytic Reduction (SCR) uses a catalyst to convert nitrogen oxides into nitrogen and water. These technologies play a crucial role in reducing pollution and meeting emission standards in the automotive industry.</p></p>
