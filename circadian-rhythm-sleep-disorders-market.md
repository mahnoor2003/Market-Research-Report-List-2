<p><p>Circadian Rhythm Sleep Disorders are a group of conditions that disrupt the body's natural sleep-wake cycle. Delayed Sleep Phase Disorder (DSP) causes a delay in the individual's sleep schedule. Jet Lag Disorder occurs when traveling across different time zones and leads to sleep disturbances. Irregular Sleep-Wake Rhythm is characterized by multiple naps throughout the day and night. Free-Running (nonentrained) Type involves a constantly changing sleep schedule. Shift Work Disorder affects individuals who work night shifts, leading to difficulty sleeping during the day. Advanced Sleep Phase Disorder (ASP) causes individuals to sleep and wake earlier than desired.</p></p>
