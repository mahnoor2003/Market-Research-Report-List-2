<p><p>Employee Benefits Administration Software refers to technology platforms that assist companies in managing their employee benefits programs. The market for this software can be categorized into two types: Cloud-based and On-premises. Cloud-based software is hosted on remote servers and accessed via the internet. It offers flexibility, scalability, and automatic updates. On the other hand, On-premises software is installed on the company's own servers and managed internally. Both types aim to streamline and automate benefits administration processes, but differ in terms of deployment and management.</p></p>
