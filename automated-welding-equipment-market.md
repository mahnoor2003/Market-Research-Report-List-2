<p><strong>The Automated Welding Equipment Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Arc Welding</li><li>Electrogas/Electroslag Welding</li><li>Friction Welding</li><li>Laser & Electron-beam Welding</li><li>Resistance Welding</li><li>Oxy-fuel Welding</li><li>Other</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Automated welding equipment market consists of various types of welding technologies. Arc welding involves the use of an electric arc to melt and join metal pieces. Electrogas and electroslag welding are used for welding thick plates by employing molten metal. Friction welding generates heat by rubbing metal surfaces together to create a bond. Laser and electron-beam welding use high-energy beams to fuse materials. Resistance welding applies electric current to create heat for joining metals. Oxy-fuel welding employs a flame produced by the chemical reaction of oxygen and a fuel gas to melt metals. Other types include stud welding, spot welding, and plasma welding.</p></p>
