<p><ul><li>Single Stage</li><li>Multiple Stages</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The water booster pump market consists of two main types: single stage and multiple stages. A single stage pump has only one impeller that helps to increase the water pressure. This type of pump is suitable for applications where the required pressure is not too high. On the other hand, a multiple stages pump has multiple impellers, allowing it to generate higher pressure levels. This type of pump is beneficial for applications requiring high-pressure water supply, such as high-rise buildings or industrial facilities.</p></p>
