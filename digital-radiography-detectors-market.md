<p><strong>Market Segmentation</strong></p>
<p><strong>The Digital Radiography Detectors Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Indirect Flat Panel Detectors</li><li>Direct Flat Panel Detectors</li><li>Computed radiography (CR) Detectors</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Digital Radiography Detectors market can be categorized into three types: Indirect Flat Panel Detectors, Direct Flat Panel Detectors, and Computed Radiography (CR) Detectors. Indirect flat panel detectors use a scintillator to convert X-ray photons into visible light, which is then converted into electrical signals. Direct flat panel detectors directly convert X-ray photons into electrical signals using a photoconductor material. Computed radiography detectors utilize a flexible imaging plate that is exposed to X-rays and then scanned to generate digital images.</p></p>
