<p><p>The surgical suction tube market is categorized into two main types: disposable and reusable. Disposable suction tubes are designed for single use and are discarded after each procedure. They offer advantages such as convenience, reduced risk of infection, and no need for cleaning or sterilization. On the other hand, reusable suction tubes can be cleaned, sterilized, and used multiple times. They are preferred in settings where cost-effectiveness and environmental sustainability are prioritized, but they require thorough cleaning and sterilization procedures to ensure patient safety. Both types serve the purpose of effectively removing fluids and debris during surgical procedures.</p></p>
