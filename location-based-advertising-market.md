<p><ul><li>Push</li><li>Pull</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Push marketing in location-based advertising refers to the act of advertisers pushing their messages or promotional content to potential customers based on their geographical location. This method involves sending tailored advertisements directly to individuals within a specific area, using channels such as SMS, push notifications, or email. On the other hand, pull marketing in location-based advertising involves attracting customers by providing information or offers when they actively seek it out. This can be done through search engines, websites, or social media platforms, allowing users to discover relevant ads based on their interests or location.</p></p>
