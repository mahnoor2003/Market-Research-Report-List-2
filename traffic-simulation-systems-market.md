<p><strong>The Traffic Simulation Systems Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Microscopic Simulation</li><li>Mesoscopic Simulation</li><li>Macroscopic Simulation</li><li>Traffic Planning Simulation</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Traffic simulation systems are used to model and analyze the movement of vehicles on roads. There are different types of simulations available. Microscopic simulation focuses on individual vehicles, simulating precise movements and interactions. Mesoscopic simulation models traffic flow at a larger scale, considering groups of vehicles or areas. Macroscopic simulation examines overall traffic patterns and congestion on a larger scale. Traffic planning simulation is used for evaluating and optimizing transportation plans and policies. These simulations aid in understanding and predicting traffic behavior, assisting in the design and management of road networks.</p></p>
