<p><p>The aviation altimeter market consists of different types of altimeters used in aircraft for measuring altitude. Barometric altimeters rely on atmospheric pressure to determine altitude. Radio altimeters use radio waves to measure the distance between the aircraft and the ground. GNSS altimeters, such as GPS and Galileo, use satellite signals to determine altitude. Laser altimeters utilize laser beams to measure the distance between the aircraft and the ground. These altimeters play a crucial role in aviation navigation and safety by providing accurate altitude information to pilots.</p></p>
