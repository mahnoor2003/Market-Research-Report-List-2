<p><p>A CNC (Computer Numerical Control) Machining Center (4-Axis) is a highly automated machine tool that is capable of performing various operations such as milling, drilling, and tapping on a workpiece in a single setup. It incorporates a rotary table that enables the machine to rotate the workpiece in addition to the X, Y, and Z axes. This additional axis improves the efficiency and versatility of the machining process.</p><p>On the other hand, a CNC Turning Center is specifically designed for turning operations, where the workpiece is rotated while a cutting tool moves in a linear motion to remove material. This type of machine is used for cylindrical workpieces and is capable of performing operations like facing, turning, drilling, and threading.</p><p>The CNC Machining Center (4-Axis) and Turning Center market is expected to witness significant growth in the forecast period due to several factors.
