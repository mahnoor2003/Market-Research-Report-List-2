<p><p>Beer chillers are products designed to keep beer at a cold, refreshing temperature. There are two main types of beer chillers: coil type and box type. Coil type chillers consist of a coiled metal tube that is submerged in ice or cold water. When beer is passed through the coil, it rapidly cools down. Box type chillers, on the other hand, are insulated containers that can hold multiple bottles or cans of beer. Ice or cold packs are placed inside the box, which then cools the beer within. Both types of chillers provide convenient and efficient cooling solutions for beer lovers.</p></p>
